**Purpose:** Position for the new world order

#### Pillar B: Alternative Energy (Necessity Innovation)

**The Thesis:**
When traditional energy supply chains break or become weaponized, "fringe" becomes vital. Research that can't compete against cheap oil suddenly has no competition.

**Target Profile:**
- Radiant/zero-point energy research
- Tesla (Nikola) patent derivatives
- Non-traditional approaches with BRICS state backing
- Currently cheap/dismissed by Western markets
- Government strategic interest in energy independence

**Why BRICS-backed:**
- State support = survival runway through crisis
- Not dependent on Western capital markets
- Governments with existential interest in alternatives
- Currently invisible to Western investors

**Research Required:**
- Academic papers on suppressed technologies
- Patent filings in BRICS nations
- Government research programs
- Companies with state backing and real progress

#### Pillar C: Australia Resilience

**The Vision:**
Australia has the resources but is politically aligned with declining powers. Energy independence changes the equation. Sovereignty through resilience.

**Australia's Assets:**
- Uranium (world's largest reserves)
- Lithium (major deposits)
- Rare earths (underdeveloped potential)
- Solar exposure (among world's best)
- Geopolitical positioning (bridge between East/West)

**Australia's Vulnerabilities:**
- Five Eyes/AUKUS alignment
- China trade dependency
- Energy import dependency (refined fuels)
- Foreign ownership of strategic assets

**The Goal:**
Research and identify paths to Australian energy independence that don't require choosing sides. Build knowledge base that could inform policy or investment in Australian resilience.

**Capital Allocation:** 20-40% of investment capital (long-term hold)

**Success Metrics:**
| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Thesis validation | Confirmed by events |
| Entry timing | Before mainstream recognition |
| Position survival | Through volatility |
| Decade return | >5x on successful positions |

Shocks propagate through the network with latency. This creates opportunity windows.

**Example: Gallium Export Ban**
```
Day 0:   China announces gallium export controls
         │
         │ [MINERALS HUB STRESS: +0.3]
         │
Day 7:   Semiconductor manufacturers assess impact
         │
         │ [MANUFACTURING STRESS: +0.2]
         │
Day 21:  Chip production guidance revised down
         │
         │ [SECTOR IMPACT: NVDA, AMD, TSM]
         │
Day 30+: Prices adjust
         │
         │ [OPPORTUNITY WINDOW CLOSED]
```

**Our Advantage:** We see Day 0-7, position by Day 14, capture Day 21+.
